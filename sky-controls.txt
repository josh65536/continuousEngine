view navigation
	right click and drag: pan
	WASD: pan
	scroll wheel: zoom
	+,-: zoom
	home: reset view
	g: toggle grid

editing answer -- correct answer will be confirmed
	click on square: select square
	arrow keys: move selected square
	1-9: enter number
	backspace: delete number
	r: reset grid
	z: undo
	x: redo

trials
	click outside grid: trial
	middle click and drag: lots of trials
	c: change colors
	escape: clear trials
